Popular FAQ

What happens if I miss a day? expand_more
Our streak system is designed for disciplined serenity. If a daily verification is missed, your current streak resets to zero, but your historical "Consistency Score" remains. We value the habit over the number.
How does the Buddy system work? expand_more
Buddies are accountability partners. They receive a notification when you successfully verify a habit and can "attest" to your proof if visual confirmation is required. Privacy is granular; you choose what they see.
